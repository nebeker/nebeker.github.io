Zine

Published on 2026-05-03

I made a zine. That’s short for “magazine” and implies it’s a DIY project.

Art and hobbies are important. They let us use energy on things that matter to us and to the world, but aren’t jobs. The key is the pursuit of a process and enjoyment for their intrinsic value. I’ve considered myself an amateur photographer for about a decade and I keep looking for new ways to see things and share what I see. This is only one of them.

I’ve been working on a fun photography project since 2024, slowly adding to it. You can find it on Flickr: Mine - A Cat’s Perspective. Now it exists in a different format, on paper.

This is a short one, I just want to encourage the reader to make art.
